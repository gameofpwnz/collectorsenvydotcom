Rules & Guidelines

Please read carefully before participating

For Proxies

  • You must be a verified proxy via our Form and Approval Process
  • You must attend the event in person and have reliable transportation
  • Respond to all proxy requests within 24 hours of receiving them
  • Provide clear photos of items and receipts after purchase
  • Ship items within 5 business days using tracking
  • Only charge exact costs (item + tax + shipping + fee)
  • Refund any unused funds if unable to complete the transaction
  • Communicate any issues or complications immediately
  • Maintain professionalism and respect at all times
  • Failure to comply may result in removal as a Verified Proxy
  • Scams will result in dismissal from the community

For Buyers

  • Submit requests at least 48 hours before event via our Form for the event
  • Provide clear instructions and product details
  • Send 50% of the total cost upfront
  • Understand that items may sell out and cannot be guaranteed
  • Cover all costs (item, tax, shipping, tariffs, and fees)
  • If theme night ticket required, buy and transfer to Proxy
  • Respond to proxy communications in a timely manner
  • Leave feedback after successful transactions
  • Failure to comply may result in a ban from the service
  • Scams will result in dismissal from the community

Fee Structure

Understanding proxy costs

Promotional Ticketed Events - Flat Rate

  • This is an event where the promotional ticket is required for the item
  • Fixed Proxy Fee $30
  • Fee covers time, travel, and effort to attend
  • Fee does not cover item cost, promo ticket, tax, tariffs, or shipping

Multi-Item Events & Conventions - Percentage

  • Proxy Fee is $20 + 5% of item cost
  • Covers time, badge cost, travel, and effort
  • Fee does not cover item cost, tax, tariffs, or shipping
  • Scaled to reward proxies and cover multi-item pickups

Example calculations:

If you are requesting an OpTic Gaming x Texas Rangers Theme Night jersey, you would pay for the Theme Ticket via the Texas Rangers + the $30 flat Proxy fee + shipping + tariffs if applicable.

If you are requesting multiple items at a convention totaling $400, you would pay $400 + $20 + ($400 x 0.05) + shipping + tariffs if applicable.

All fees should be discussed and agreed upon before the proxy attends the event. Buyers are responsible for all costs including the item, any promotional tickets, applicable taxes/tariffs, proxy fee, and shipping. Most proxies accept PayPal Goods & Services for buyer protection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our proxy service

How do I know my proxy will actually get the item? Items at conventions and events can sell out quickly. While our verified proxies will do their best to secure your items, availability cannot be guaranteed. We recommend having backup options and communicating priorities clearly.

When should I send payment? A down payment of 50% of the estimated cost is typically required. You may negotiate with your Proxy. Always use payment methods with buyer protection.

What if the item is damaged during shipping? Proxies should package items securely and provide tracking. If damage occurs during shipping, this is typically a matter between the buyer and shipping carrier. Discuss insurance options with your proxy for high-value items.

Can I request multiple items from one proxy? Yes! Most proxies are happy to pick up multiple items. Be sure to provide a prioritized list in case everything isn't available. Review Structured Fee section.

What happens if there's a dispute? We encourage open communication between buyers and proxies to resolve any issues. However, Collectors Envy is a facilitation platform only. We are not responsible for disputes, lost items, payment issues, or any problems that arise during transactions.

I bought an item, but it ended up cheaper after the event. Can I get a refund? No, all sales are final. Proxies purchase items based on the information available at the time. Any price changes after the fact are not the responsibility of the proxy. Proxies are not responsible for market fluctuations.

I live in another country that the Proxy. Can I still use this service? Yes, just ensure that the Proxy you are using is willing to ship internationally. Be aware of any customs duties, tariffs, or shipping costs that may apply.

I don't want to pay a proxy fee. Why do I have to? Proxy fees cover the time, effort, and costs incurred by the proxy to attend events and secure items. They are not optional and should be factored into your budget when requesting proxy services. Over time, you may develop a relationship with a Proxy and could negotiate deals. However, you are not required to use this service. You can use a friend or buy resale after an event. This is just a platform for convenience.
